How it works

From “applying by hand” to “applied while you sleep”

Set it up once, then let the agent do the repetitive work on every job board.

1

Install & set your profile

Load the extension in Chrome or Edge and fill in your details, resume answers, and job preferences once. Add your own Gemini key if you want AI-written answers.

2

Open a search & hit Start

Go to LinkedIn, Indeed, Naukri or Bayt, run a job search, and press Start. Tick specific jobs to apply only to those, or let it apply to every match.

3

Watch it apply

The agent opens each job, fills the form, answers questions, clicks through every step, submits, and moves on — page after page, until you stop it.

Supported platforms

One agent, four job boards

Each platform has its own dedicated apply flow, tuned to how that site really works.

Easy Apply

LinkedIn — end to end

Detects every Easy Apply job in your search, opens the modal, steps through each page of the application, fills answers and uploads, then submits — and paginates to the next results page automatically.

Apply with Indeed

Indeed — multi-step apply

Handles the full Apply-with-Indeed flow, the cross-domain hop to the apply page, and every Continue / Submit step. If a captcha appears it hands off to you, then resumes on its own.

Naukri & Naukri Gulf

Naukri — full application flow

Clicks Apply, walks through chatbot or direct-form questions, answers them from your saved profile, and returns to the list to continue. Naukri.com and Naukri Gulf each run as their own flow.

Easy Apply

Bayt — Easy Apply

Scans the results list, clicks Easy Apply on each job, fills the application and any screening questions, clicks “Apply now”, then returns to the search and continues through every page.

FAQ

Questions, answered

Which job sites are supported?

LinkedIn (Easy Apply), Indeed, Naukri and Naukri Gulf, and Bayt. Each has its own dedicated apply flow.

Which browsers does it work on?

Google Chrome and Microsoft Edge. It’s a Manifest V3 extension you load unpacked — download the zip, unzip it, and add it from the extensions page.

Do I have to sit and watch it?

No. Once you press Start it runs on its own across pages and job listings. It only pauses for captchas, which it flags for you and resumes automatically once solved.

Is it safe to use on my account?

It runs entirely inside your own browser with your own login, uses human-like pacing, and never auto-solves captchas. You stay in control and can stop it at any moment.

How do I get access?

Sign in and enter your activation key — or buy a plan for instant access. The same key unlocks both the extension and the CRM tracker.

Install & activate

1

Download the zip above and unzip it anywhere on your computer.

2

Open chrome://extensions (or edge://extensions) and turn on Developer mode (top-right).

3

Click Load unpacked and select the unzipped extension folder.

4

Click the 🧩 puzzle icon in the toolbar → open the extension → Prefs tab.

5

Paste your activation key (shown in the Activation Key box above) and click Save & Activate — it should say “✓ Activated”.

6

Open LinkedIn Jobs, Indeed, Naukri, or Bayt, fill your profile, and hit Start.
