Installation Guide

1

Download the zip above and extract it anywhere on your computer.

2

Open chrome://extensions (or edge://extensions) and enable Developer mode in the top-right corner.

3

Click Load unpacked and select the extracted extension folder.

4

Open LinkedIn Jobs, Indeed, or Naukri — click the AutoApplier icon, fill your profile, and hit Start.
