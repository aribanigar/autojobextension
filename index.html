Auto apply to jobs on LinkedIn, Indeed, Naukri and Bayt

AutoApplier is a browser extension that does your job applications for you. You run a normal search on any supported job board, press Start, and the agent opens each listing, fills in the application, answers the screening questions from your saved profile, submits it, and moves straight on to the next job. You stay in control and can stop it at any time.

One agent, four job boards

Every platform has its own dedicated apply flow, built around how that site actually works.

LinkedIn Easy Apply

LinkedIn, end to end

The agent finds every Easy Apply job in your search, opens the application modal, and steps through each page. It fills your answers, handles the dropdowns and questions, and submits. Then it closes the modal, moves to the next card, and turns the page automatically so it keeps going without you.

Apply with Indeed

Indeed, multi step apply

Indeed applications span several steps and often move to a separate apply page. The agent follows that whole flow, clicking Continue and Submit through each stage. If a captcha appears it never tries to solve it. It spotlights the captcha, alerts you, and picks up again the moment you clear it.

Naukri and Naukri Gulf

Naukri, full application flow

The agent clicks Apply, then works through the questions whether they appear as a chatbot or as a direct form, answering each one from your saved profile. When the application is done it returns to the list and continues. Naukri.com and Naukri Gulf each run as their own separate flow.

Bayt Easy Apply

Bayt, Easy Apply

On Bayt the agent scans the results list, clicks Easy Apply on each job, and fills in the application along with any screening questions. It clicks Apply now to submit, returns to the job search, and keeps going through every listing and every page of results.

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before you start.

Which job sites does it support?

AutoApplier works on LinkedIn Easy Apply, Indeed, Naukri, Naukri Gulf, and Bayt. Each site has its own dedicated apply flow that is tuned to how that platform actually behaves.

Which browsers can I use?

Google Chrome and Microsoft Edge. AutoApplier is a Manifest V3 extension that you load unpacked. You download the zip, unzip it, and add it from your browser extensions page. The dashboard walks you through every step.

Do I have to sit and watch it work?

No. Once you press Start it runs on its own across pages and job listings. The only time it pauses is for a captcha, which it flags for you and then resumes automatically once you have solved it.

Is it safe to use on my accounts?

The agent runs entirely inside your own browser using your own login. It uses human like timing, never auto solves captchas, and does nothing you could not do yourself. You stay in full control and can stop it instantly at any moment.

Can I choose which jobs to apply to?

Yes. You can let the agent apply to every job that matches your search, or tick only the specific listings you want. When you tick jobs, it applies to just those, one after another, in the order you picked them.

How do I get access?

Sign in and enter your activation key, or buy a plan for instant access. The same key unlocks both the extension and the built in CRM tracker, so you only manage one key.
