JobBot

An auto-apply agent that fills forms, clicks Continue, and moves through job listings for you.

⬇ Download Extension v1.0.0 · Chrome / Edge / Brave (Manifest V3) · Open CRM dashboard →

What it does

📝

Smart form filling

Detects questions about experience, salary, notice period, relocation, and more — and answers from your saved profile.

🖱️

Auto-navigates steps

Clicks Continue, Review, and Submit through multi-step application flows, then moves to the next job.

👀

Visible while it works

A spotlight overlay highlights every field it fills and button it clicks, with a stop button always on screen.

📊

Stats & history

Tracks applications per platform with a badge counter and keeps a history of the last 500 jobs applied to.

Install in 4 steps

Download the zip above and extract it anywhere on your computer.
Open chrome://extensions in Chrome (or edge://extensions in Edge) and turn on Developer mode in the top-right corner.
Click Load unpacked and select the extracted extension folder.
Open LinkedIn Jobs, Indeed, or Naukri, click the JobBot icon, fill in your profile, and hit Start.
⚠️ Use responsibly. Automated activity may violate the terms of service of LinkedIn, Indeed, or Naukri and can lead to account restrictions. Review every application setting carefully and use JobBot at your own risk.
