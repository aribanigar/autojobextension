What is Naukri auto apply?

Naukri auto apply means automating applications on Naukri.com (and Naukri Gulf for the Middle East). AutoApplier clicks Apply on matching roles, answers the Naukri chatbot questions — notice period, current and expected CTC, experience, location — from your profile, remembers answers you fill manually so it can reuse them, and continues across the results so you apply to far more roles in less time.

How AutoApplier works on Naukri

  1. 1
    Search on Naukri as usual

    Run your normal Naukri or Naukri Gulf search with your filters. Nothing new to learn.

  2. 2
    Press Start

    Open AutoApplier and hit Start. It begins applying to matching roles one by one.

  3. 3
    It answers the Naukri chatbot

    Notice period, current CTC, expected CTC, total experience and location questions are answered from your profile.

  4. 4
    It learns and reuses answers

    Any question you answer manually is remembered and auto-filled the next time a similar question appears.

  5. 5
    It keeps applying

    Each application is logged and the agent continues through the listings until you stop it.

Frequently asked questions

Can AutoApplier auto apply on Naukri.com?

Yes. AutoApplier applies to matching Naukri roles and answers the Naukri chatbot screening questions — notice period, current and expected CTC, experience and location — from your saved profile.

Does it support Naukri Gulf?

Yes. Naukri Gulf is supported as a separate platform for Middle East roles, using the same profile and learned answers.

Does it remember my Naukri answers?

Yes. Any question you fill manually is saved and auto-filled next time a similar question appears, so screening questions get faster over time.

Is Naukri auto apply safe for my account?

The agent runs inside your own browser using your own login, with human-like timing, and never auto-solves captchas. You stay in control and can stop instantly.

Which browsers does it need?

Chrome or Edge. AutoApplier is a Manifest V3 extension loaded unpacked from your extensions page.
