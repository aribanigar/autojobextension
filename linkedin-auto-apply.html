What is LinkedIn auto apply?

LinkedIn auto apply means letting software complete LinkedIn Easy Apply applications on your behalf. Instead of clicking into every posting and re-typing the same details, AutoApplier reads each Easy Apply modal, maps its fields to your profile, handles the multi-step Next / Review / Submit flow, and applies to job after job across every results page until you tell it to stop.

How AutoApplier works on LinkedIn

  1. 1
    Search on LinkedIn as usual

    Open LinkedIn Jobs, apply your filters (title, location, Easy Apply), and get your results list — exactly how you search today.

  2. 2
    Press Start

    Open AutoApplier and hit Start. The agent begins opening Easy Apply jobs one by one, with human-like timing.

  3. 3
    It fills and submits

    Name, email, phone, experience, notice period, work authorization and screening questions are answered from your saved profile — including custom questions it learns and reuses.

  4. 4
    It clicks through every step

    The multi-page Easy Apply flow (Next → Review → Submit) is handled automatically, resume step included.

  5. 5
    It moves to the next job

    After each submit it paginates and keeps going, logging every application in the built-in tracker.

Frequently asked questions

Does AutoApplier work with LinkedIn Easy Apply?

Yes. LinkedIn Easy Apply is fully supported. The agent handles the multi-step Easy Apply modal — filling fields, answering screening questions, selecting a resume, and clicking Next, Review and Submit.

Is it safe to auto apply on LinkedIn?

The agent runs entirely inside your own browser using your own login, with human-like timing. It never auto-solves captchas and does nothing you could not do yourself. You stay in full control and can stop it instantly.

Can I choose which LinkedIn jobs it applies to?

Yes. Let it apply to every job that matches your search, or tick only the specific listings you want and it applies to just those, in the order you picked.

Do I have to watch it apply?

No. Once you press Start it runs on its own across pages and listings. It only pauses for a captcha, which it flags and then resumes automatically once solved.

Which browsers does the LinkedIn auto apply extension support?

Google Chrome and Microsoft Edge. AutoApplier is a Manifest V3 extension you load unpacked — download the zip, unzip, and add it from your browser extensions page.
